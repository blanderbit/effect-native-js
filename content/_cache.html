Profile
Norgram® is founded in 2016 by Sebastian Gram & Mathias Høst Normark. Located in Aarhus & Copenhagen, Denmark.
Together we have over 20 years of industry experience and have had the opportunity to work with everything from small startups, museums like Guggenheim and MoMA to large corporations such as Google and NASA. We are both former senior level designers from Digital Creative Agency — Hello Monday, where we’ve worked both from Denmark and also lived in New York for a few years. Our vision is to form meaningful collaborations with brands, start-ups and organisations in building the next generation of digital brands through digital identity, digital platforms and experiences.



We are digital natives.
We are a Design Studio for the Digital Era.
We are Norgram® the Design Director duo of internationally recognized and awarded designers Sebastian Gram & Mathias Høst Normark. Our vision is to collaborate with brands in building the next generation of digital brands through digital identities, experiences & platforms.
